# Deaths in Iceland

Deaths and mid-year populations in Iceland, by age, sex, and calendar
year.

## Usage

``` r
isl_deaths
```

## Format

A [tibble](https://tibble.tidyverse.org/reference/tibble-package.html)
with 5,300 rows and the following columns:

- `age` Single year of age, up to `"105+"`

- `sex` `"Female"` and `"Male"`

- `time` Calendar year, 1998-2022

- `deaths` Counts of deaths

- `popn` Mid-year population

## Source

Tables "Deaths by municipalities, sex and age 1981-2022", and "Average
annual population by municipality, age and sex 1998-2022 - Current
municipalities", on the Statistics Iceland website. Data downloaded on
12 July 2023.
